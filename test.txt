
About Me.

A Full Stack Developer with 5+ years of experience in developing enterprise applications. Currently working as a Team Lead & Senior Software Engineer at Red Hat.

I spend most of my leisure time contributing to open source projects. I had contributed to Mozilla. DuckDuckGo and several other open source organizations. To know more about my contributions you can visit my GitHub profile.

I have hands-on experience in working on several projects that use Python, Node.Js, PHP, Java, etc. Most of my projects are released as open-source on GitHub. You can find more details about my projects in the portfolio section of this website or my Github profile.

Got an offer for me? Reach me at hello@gautamkrishnar.com
